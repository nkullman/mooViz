MOO Viz: Multi-objective optimization visualization

MOO Viz exists to help visualize and understand Pareto-optimal point sets, specifically those produced by the solutions of multi-objective math programming models.

To compare these point sets (frontiers) MOO Viz can produce a handful of visualization aids, such as tables, scatterplots, and parallel coordinate plots.

MOO Viz also computes metrics that describe the competition between frontiers in a dataset and between objectives within a frontier. For the former, we use metrics from evolutionary multi-objective optimization like the hypervolume indicator. For the latter, we use a custom conflict metric, which you can read about in our upcoming publication.

For more views, try switching between viz aids in the Settings panel.

UNDER CONSTRUCTION

MOO Viz is currently under construction. At the moment, only frontier statistics and a couple viz aids are available for display. We will be adding in more viz aids and more features as time goes on.
